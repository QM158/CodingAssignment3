Banana Split Sundae

Recipe source: Created by Quinn based on personal kitchen experiments and classic inspiration

Ingredients
  • 1 ripe banana
  • 3 scoops of ice cream (vanilla, chocolate, strawberry)
  • Whipped cream
  • Chocolate syrup
  • Crushed nuts
  • Maraschino cherry
  • Sprinkles (optional)
Equipment
  • Long sundae dish
  • Ice cream scooper
  • Butter knife
  • Spoon
Directions
  1. Peel the banana and slice it in half lengthwise. Place both halves in the dish.
  2. Add three scoops of ice cream in a row between the banana halves.
  3. Drizzle chocolate syrup over the top.
  4. Add whipped cream generously to each scoop.
  5. Sprinkle crushed nuts and optional sprinkles over the whipped cream.
  6. Top each scoop with a maraschino cherry and serve immediately.
